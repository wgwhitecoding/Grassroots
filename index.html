Our Vision

Grassroots To Work is on a mission to ensure that every demographic has access to quality careers guidance provision and access to the Best work experience for the basis of career growth and exploration.

This is achieved through quality programming & services that close the gap between education and employment or transitions from unemployment to employment.

Careers Services

  • CV Refinement
  • Career Assessments
  • Interview Support
  • College/University Application Preparation
  • Career Switch or Transitions
  • Careers Counselling
